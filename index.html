Onyx Control

Privacy Policy — Last updated: March 2026

Onyx Control respects your privacy. This policy explains how the app handles your data.

1. Data Collection

Onyx Control does NOT collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers. The app operates entirely on your local network.

2. Local Storage

The app stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Apple TV pairing credentials (encrypted using Android Keystore)
  • App preferences (display order, favorites)
  • Cached app icon URLs from the Apple iTunes API

This data never leaves your device except as described below.

3. Network Communication

The app communicates over your local Wi-Fi network with Apple TV devices using the Apple Companion Protocol. Additionally, the app contacts Apple's iTunes Search API (itunes.apple.com) to fetch app icon images. No personal or identifying information is sent in these requests.

4. Third-Party Services

The app does not integrate any analytics, advertising, or tracking services. The only external service contacted is Apple's public iTunes Search API for retrieving app artwork.

5. Permissions

  • Network permissions: Required to discover and communicate with Apple TV devices on your local network
  • Foreground service: Required to maintain connection while the app is in the background
  • Notifications: Used to display Now Playing information

6. Children's Privacy

The app does not knowingly collect data from children.

7. Changes

This policy may be updated. Changes will be reflected in the app's store listing.

8. Contact
